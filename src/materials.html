Materials & Technologies

Efficient molding technology
AZUMI uses an efficient molding technology that allows to get the perfect folds of the filter material, which allows to distribute the airflow as evenly as possible and maintain its stability.
Filter housings
AZUMI uses durable, but flexible, temperature and wear resistant materials to produce filter housings.
High-quality gaskets
All AZUMI filters have high-quality gaskets and are frost resistant and highly efficient at low emperatures, reducing the leak risks.
Quality filter material
A quality filter material that combines density for efficient filtration with good air and oil throughput.
